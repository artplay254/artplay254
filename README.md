Since I was 12, I've been in a permanent experimenting mode. Blender, Arduino, parkour, calisthenics, martial arts, BMX tricks, Linux, cold showers, 5 AM mornings — I try everything, and I keep what makes me better. I don't like easy. When there's a hard task in the room, I want it.

I value depth over noise. I'd rather discuss a crazy universe theory at 1 AM than scroll TikTok for three hours. I train my body, I train my mind, and I try not to waste either.
